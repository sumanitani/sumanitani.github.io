Research Focus

1. LLMs for Magnetic Property Prediction

Using Qwen-14B and Mistral-7B models, I am fine-tuning Large Language Models to interpret chemical compositions and predict Curie temperatures. This involves creating custom tokenizers for chemical formulas and utilizing the Unsloth library for efficient training on consumer GPUs.

3. Reinforcement Learning in Material Science

Applying Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) and GRPO to optimize material structures for specific magnetic traits.
