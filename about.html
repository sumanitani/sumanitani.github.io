Biography

I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Physics at the University of New Hampshire. My work bridges the gap between condensed matter physics and modern machine learning.


Prior to joining UNH, I completed my BSc and MSc at Tribhuwan University. My current focus is leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs) and graph neural networks to predict magnetic and superconducting properties of novel materials.
